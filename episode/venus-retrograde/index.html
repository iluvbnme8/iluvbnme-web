Venus Retrograde & Rewriting Your Worth

Venus turns inward and so do we, revisiting old stories about love, money, and what we believe we deserve.

Show notes

  • What Venus retrograde actually stirs up in love and money.
  • The old worth-stories that resurface right about now.
  • A journaling ritual to catch and rewrite them.
  • This week's card: the Four of Cups.

Transcript

[Cold open]

Has an ex crossed your mind out of nowhere this week? Have you found yourself questioning what you're worth, in love, in money, in that room where you always shrink? You might not be spiraling. Venus might just be retrograde.

[Theme music in, under]

Hey love, welcome back to iLuvBnMe. I'm Stefy, and today Venus, the planet of love, beauty, money, and worth, has turned to walk backward for a while. So let's talk about what she is here to help us rewrite.

Segment one — what Venus retrograde actually does

About every eighteen months, Venus appears to move backward through the sky for around six weeks. And because Venus rules how we love and what we value, this is a season where all of that turns inward. Old flames resurface. Old insecurities about money and desirability get loud again. It can feel a little haunted, honestly. But the ghosts are showing up for a reason: Venus is handing you your old worth-stories so you can finally revise them.

Segment two — the stories we inherited

Here's what I mean by worth-stories. Somewhere along the way, most of us absorbed a quiet equation. "I'm lovable when I'm useful." "I'm worth more when I'm smaller." "I have to earn rest, earn love, earn taking up space." You didn't choose these. You inherited them, from culture, from family, from that one relationship that taught you to keep the peace by disappearing. Venus retrograde turns the volume up on them precisely so you can hear them clearly enough to talk back.

Your worth was never up for negotiation. You just learned to bargain with something that was always yours.

Segment three — the rewrite ritual

So grab a journal, because we're going to rewrite one story tonight.

[Music out — just voice]

Draw a line down the middle of the page. On the left, finish this sentence honestly: "I feel worthy when..." Write whatever comes, even the ugly conditional stuff. "When I'm thin." "When I'm producing." "When everyone's happy with me." Get it all out. Then, on the right side, for each one, write the truer version: "I am worthy, and..." "I am worthy, and I'm allowed to rest." "I am worthy, and I don't have to perform to be loved." Read the right side out loud. That's the rewrite. Do it as many nights as you need.

The card of the week

I pulled the Four of Cups for us, a person being offered a fresh cup they can't quite see yet because they're so focused on what's gone. Let Venus retrograde be the tap on the shoulder. Grieve what's behind you, yes. But don't miss the new love, the new worth, being offered right in front of you.

Before you go

If this cracked something open, be extra gentle with yourself these next few weeks. The free Self-Love Starter Kit is at the link to help you begin, and we do deep worth-work together inside Frequency Shift. Share this with the friend who keeps forgetting how rare she is.

[Outro music]

Until next time, remember: you don't earn your worth. You were born already holding it. I love you. Bye for now.
