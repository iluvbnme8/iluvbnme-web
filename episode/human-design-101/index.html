Segment one — what even is Human Design

So, simplest possible version. Human Design is a system that blends a few older frameworks, astrology, the chakras, the I Ching, into one chart based on your exact birth date, time, and place. But you don't need to understand any of the machinery to get value from it. Think of it less like a horoscope and more like a personality map that focuses on one incredibly useful thing: how your energy is actually designed to work.

Segment two — the five types

There are five energy types, and knowing yours is the doorway to everything else. Let me give you the one-line version of each.

Generators and Manifesting Generators, that's most of us, have steady, sustainable life-force energy, and you're designed to respond to life, to light up when the right thing shows up. Projectors are here to guide and to see others deeply; you're not built to grind, you're built to be invited. Manifestors are the initiators, the ones who start things and then let others catch up. And Reflectors, the rarest, are the mirrors, deeply sensitive to the health of the world around them.

If you don't know yours yet, pause this, look up a free Human Design chart, pop in your birth info, and come back. I'll wait.

Segment three — strategy is the whole game

Here's the part that genuinely changed my life. Each type has a "strategy," basically, the right way for you to engage with life so you stop hitting walls. For Generators, it's to wait to respond instead of forcing. For Projectors, it's to wait for the invitation. For Manifestors, it's to inform before you act. For Reflectors, it's to give yourself a full lunar cycle before big decisions.

You were never behind. You were just running on someone else's strategy.

When you start honoring your strategy, the theme is the same for everyone: less resistance. Fewer walls. Things start to feel like they're flowing toward you instead of you dragging them uphill.

Your experiment this week

I don't want you to believe any of this on faith. Human Design is an experiment. So this week, just try your strategy once. If you're a Generator, wait for something to genuinely excite you before you say yes. If you're a Projector, wait to be asked before you offer your brilliant advice. Notice what happens in your body. That is your data.
