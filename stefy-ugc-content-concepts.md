# Stefy UGC Content Concepts

Creator tone: soft-spoken, warm, relatable, calm, and easy to trust.

Filming style: natural light, clean background, close-up hands, calm voiceover, gentle facial reactions, clear product shots, and simple captions.

Important note for wellness/intimate care: keep language focused on routine, freshness, comfort, confidence, and personal experience. Avoid medical claims, cure language, or promising health outcomes.

## Quick Filming Checklist

- Film near a window with soft natural light.
- Wipe products before close-ups.
- Capture 3 angles per product: wide, medium, close-up.
- Record 5-10 extra B-roll clips for every product.
- Keep hooks under 3 seconds.
- Use simple on-screen text.
- Avoid saying products cure, treat, prevent, or guarantee results.
- End every portfolio demo with your email or handle.
